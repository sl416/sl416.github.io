Sleep

I was chosen to showcase my web application, Sleep, at the New York City Computer Science Opportunity Fair, New York City's largest annual college and career inspiration event for public high school students studying computer science. My web application aims to spread awareness about insomnia and helps users sleep better through music therapy. Users are also able to create an account to save and edit their information, calculate and record how long they've slept, save music/nature sounds to their favorites, and learn more about insomnia/sleep. I used HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Node.js to create this web application.

The CS Fair hosted over 2,000 students from 60 schools across all five boroughs as well as 100+ companies, colleges and extra-curricular programs. 10 teams were selected for the Student Showcase. Students accepted into the Student Showcase received: 2 tickets to Virtual Reality World NYC including transportation, a meet-and-greet session with tech industry VIPs, appreciation packs with tech company swag, and special recognition from companies and colleges at the fair.
